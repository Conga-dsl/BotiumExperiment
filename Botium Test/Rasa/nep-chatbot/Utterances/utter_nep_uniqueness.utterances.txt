MHRD has been renamed as Ministry of Education Higher Education Commission of India (HECI) will be set up as a single umbrella body for the entire higher education, excluding medical and legal education. HECI will be having four independent verticals namely, • National Higher Education Regulatory Council (NHERC) for regulation, • General Education Council (GEC) for standard setting, • Higher Education Grants Council (HEGC) for funding, • National Accreditation Council (NAC) for accreditation.
